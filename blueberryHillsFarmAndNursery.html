Blueberry Hills Farm

Blueberry Hills Farm & Nursery offers a large selection of flowers and blueberries. They also offer pies and jams featuring their blueberries.

After purchasing a blueberry farm in 2012, we set up a high tunnel and plowed gardens. Today, we raise turnips, green beans, yellow beans, basil, swiss chard, sweet potatoes, potatoes, carrots, beets, blueberries, kale, lettuce, mustard greens, turnip greens, dill, tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, peppers, summer squash, butternut squash, corn, okra, spinach and radishes.
